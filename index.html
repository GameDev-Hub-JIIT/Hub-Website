Welcome to Game Dev Hub

The Games Dev Society is an exciting society at JIIT for people who share a love of games and development! If you'd love to meet like-minded people at monthly socials where you can just chill and play some games, or if you're after creating something new to add to your portfolio by participating in some game jams then we're the society for you! We also run workshops to get you ahead in programming engines such as Unreal and Unity and animators like 3DS Max, Blender, and some other programs.
